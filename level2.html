CRICKET BAT

A cricket bat is a key piece of equipment used in the sport of cricket.
It’s typically made from willow wood, particularly English or Kashmir willow, due to its lightness and durability.
The bat features a flat, broad blade and a long handle.
It’s used to strike the ball when batting

CRICKET ball

A cricket ball is a hard, solid ball used in the game of cricket.
It is traditionally made of a cork core wrapped in tightly wound string and covered with a leather casing.
The ball is typically red for Test matches and white for limited-overs formats like ODIs and T20s
